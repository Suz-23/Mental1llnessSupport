What is Depression

Depression is a mental illness where a person have long term feeling of sadness or loss of interest. Though many people think that depression is something to not take seriously, it could still lead to major conflicts like suicide or self-harm. It is also indicated that about 7% of adults in united states experiences depression every year.

Though some factors that contribute to its developement is imbalace of neoutransmitters, like serotonin and dopamine. Genetices, if a person has a relative with depression, it is indicated that the person will have three times as likely to develop depression. Or Stressful events like divorce, isolation, or lack of support from others. And some medical conditions such as, diabete or medications could cause depression as a side effect.
