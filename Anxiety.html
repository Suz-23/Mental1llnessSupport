What is Anxiety Disorder?

Anxiety disorder is when a feeling of stress, fear, uneaseness and dredindness, which is categorized to be anxiety, continues for few monthes, or even few years. Anxiety could appear to anyone, for example, someone could have a sudden feeling of stress or fear, before taking a test, or feel overwhelmed after getting loads of work or homeworks. Though Anxiety disorder is when these feelings of Anxiety continues for a long period of time.
