What is Mental Illness?

Mental Illness is a health condition in changes of emotions, thinking, and behaviors. It can be associated with distress, and problems in family, work, and also social gatherings. In a recent reasearch on mental illnesses, it is stated that there 23.1% of the adults in the United States live with mental illness. It was also shown that people in the age range of 18-25 years old experience the most metal illnesses, at 36.2%.
