Collective Solutions

The collective solutions for the inequallities towards all mental illnesses, is educate people about mental illnesses more, by using social media, for example a social media campaign that educates, or raises awareness about mental illness. Or a special class, which focuses on mental illnesses. But also have an awareness that mental illnesses are a serious topic and not something that could easliy be gone, or treated. When our community has an understanding of the struggles that people with mental illnesses face, it will ultimately lead to a supportive, less isolated, comforting community for people with mental illnesses. In fact, according to National Allience on Mental Illness (NAMI) after schools educating and rising awareness about mental illnesses, less students reported to feel isolated in their community. Proving that by educating, or rising awareness, and showing support towards mental illness, more people feel apart of the community, instead of isolation. And since not getting the support people need or want is the inequality that the people with mental illness face, educating people is the best way to end this enquality.
